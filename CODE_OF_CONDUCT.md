# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone interested in this research. Participation in this project should be a positive experience regardless of age, gender, disability, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, level of experience, or academic background.

## Expected Behaviour

Contributors and participants are encouraged to:

* Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
* Welcome different viewpoints and technical opinions.
* Focus on improving the quality and reproducibility of the research.
* Report bugs and issues with clear, reproducible examples.
* Provide feedback in a professional and courteous manner.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours will not be tolerated:

* Harassment or discriminatory language.
* Personal attacks or abusive comments.
* Publishing private or confidential information.
* Deliberate disruption of discussions or collaboration.
* Any behaviour that creates an unprofessional or hostile environment.

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter inappropriate behaviour or have concerns regarding interactions within this repository, please open a private GitHub issue or contact the repository maintainer directly.

All reports will be reviewed respectfully and handled appropriately.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Any communication directly related to this research repository

## Research Ethics

This repository accompanies academic research on explainable artificial intelligence for neural cryptanalysis. Contributors are encouraged to maintain high standards of scientific integrity by:

* Reporting experimental results honestly.
* Clearly documenting methodological changes.
* Ensuring that proposed improvements are reproducible.
* Properly acknowledging prior work and citations.

## Attribution

This project builds upon the DeepSpeck neural cryptanalysis framework introduced by **A. Gohr (CRYPTO 2019)**. Contributors should continue to acknowledge foundational work while clearly distinguishing any new contributions made within this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, discussions, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct in order to maintain a respectful and productive research environment.
